Photo Guidelines for Best Results

Good Lighting

Use natural daylight or bright indoor lighting. Avoid shadows and harsh flash.

Proper Distance

Keep camera 6-12 inches away. Include the entire lesion plus some surrounding skin.

Clear Focus

Hold the camera steady. Make sure the image is sharp and not blurry.

Good Framing

Center the lesion in the photo. Include a reference object (coin, ruler) if possible.

Note: Better photo quality leads to more accurate analysis results.
